The Gist

A dashboard where companies can practice transparency
and consumers can find products that align with their ideals.

This is a special project for me. Inspired by my love of nature, fashion and the need for
for action when it comes to the climate crisis. It's my belief that small step in the right direction would be
for companies to subscribe to a system for cataloging and monitoring their impacts
on the environment. And for consumers to have a centralized place where they can go
to educate themselves on the impacts of the goods they buy.
